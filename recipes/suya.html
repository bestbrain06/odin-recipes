West African Grilled Beef (Suya)

This is my take on Suya, grilled beef skewers from West Africa. Sirloin steak is marinated in a quick and easy marinade that is extremely delicious and perfect for cooking on the grill.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon finely ground roasted peanuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 ½ pounds beef tri-tip steak, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ¼ onion, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 4 mushrooms, halved
